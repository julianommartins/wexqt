WEX-WS Filters HELP

Filters are CASE SENSITIVE: ('JAPAN' <> 'Japan' , $BAND <> band)

Filters names must have $ to be defined: $BAND, $WORK_LOCATION_IOT

Examples:

$PRACTITIONER_ID='1234456'

($BAND='7' or $BAND='8') -- will search for practitioners that Have band 7 or 8

($BAND='7' or $BAND='8') and $WORK_LOCATION_CITY='Virginia' -- the above + people from Virginia City

$OPPORTUNITY_REVENUE=0

boolean($EMAIL_RECIPIENT) -- will show just results that HAVE EMAIL_RECIPIENT

not($EMAIL_RECIPIENT) -- will show just results that DO NOT HAVE EMAIL_RECIPIENT
